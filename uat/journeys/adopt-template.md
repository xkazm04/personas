## Goal (user POV)
"I don't want to build from scratch — show me something close to my job and let me make it mine in minutes."

## Definition of done
- I found a template that matches my job, understood what it does, and adopted it into a Persona that's wired up (or clearly tells me the one thing left to wire).
- It didn't dump me into a half-configured agent that silently fails on first run.

## What L1 must check
- Gallery → preview → adopt → working-persona path; does adoption wire event subscriptions + connectors, or leave gaps?
- Is the gallery legible to a non-technical user (categories, "what this does")?

## What L2 must confirm (l2_priority)
- Does an adopted template actually run end-to-end, or hit a missing-credential / unwired-trigger wall?
- Time from "browsing" to "first successful run".
