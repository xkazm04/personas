## Goal (user POV)
"I don't want to learn the app. I want to tell an assistant what I need and have it figure out the building/running for me."

## Definition of done
- I described a job in chat; the companion either did it or built/ran the right thing — and walked me through what it was doing.
- It used my real context (my connectors, my data), not a canned answer.

## What L1 must check
- The companion's tool surface: can it actually build/run/assign, or only chat?
- Grounding: does the companion turn receive my personas/connectors/goals, or thin context?

## What L2 must confirm (l2_priority)
- A real companion turn against a real prompt: does it act, and is the action correct + grounded (names my supplied entity)?
- Conversation responsiveness vs. the serialized-turn wait (patience economics).
