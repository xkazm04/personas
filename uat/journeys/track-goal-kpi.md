## Goal (user POV)
"I want outcomes, not activity. Set a goal/KPI and show me whether the automation is actually moving it."

## Definition of done
- I defined a goal/KPI, connected it to what the agents do, and saw a measurement that reflects reality.
- It distinguishes "ran a lot" from "moved the number".

## What L1 must check
- Goal/KPI define → measure → derive path; is it wired to executions, or a standalone form?
- Reachability/tier; is this a power-user-only surface?

## What L2 must confirm (l2_priority)
- A real measurement appears and is grounded in actual run data.
- Whether the outcome framing reads as real or as a dashboard placebo.
