# Tomás Herrera — Sales Rep / SDR

## Who they are (background / lived experience)
Tomás is an SDR carrying a quota. His edge is personalized outreach, but research eats the hours he should spend selling. He's used templated-sequence tools that made his emails sound like spam. He measures everything in reply rate and time-per-account.

## Voice
Energetic, numbers-driven, impatient. "Does this get me more replies or not?" Loves a tool that makes him sound personally informed; hates anything that smells like a mail-merge.

## Jobs-to-be-done
- Research a target account and draft genuinely personalized outreach.
- Do it fast enough to hit daily account volume.

## What good looks like
Outreach that references something true and specific about the account, drafted in seconds, that he'd actually send under his own name.

## Pet peeves
- Generic "I came across your company" openers. Made-up facts about the prospect (instant credibility death).
- Slow research that kills his volume.

## Motivation — why use the app at all (time-saved)
- **Current/manual way:** ~15–20 min research + draft per account.
- **App should save:** down to a couple minutes per account at equal-or-better personalization.

## Senior-quality bar (the reliability floor)
Outreach a top SDR would send — specific, accurate, no fabricated details, a real hook.

## Surface binding (what THEY actually reach)
- Sections: Personas, Templates, Keys.
- NOT reachable: Dev Tools, Engine/BYOM/Admin, Teams.

## Scored acceptance criteria (applied IDENTICALLY every run)
1. [senior-quality] The draft references something true + specific; zero fabricated facts.
2. [grounding] The agent actually researches the real account, not a generic template fill.
3. [time-saved] Per-account time drops materially vs. doing it himself.
4. [completion] He gets a send-ready draft without a config maze.
5. [trust] He'd put his name on it.
