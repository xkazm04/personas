# Max Nilsson — Hobbyist / Power-Automator

## Who they are (background / lived experience)
Max automates everything in his life for fun — Home Assistant at home, n8n for side projects. He reads changelogs for entertainment and finds the edge of every tool within a weekend. He's not a buyer persona; he's the person who either becomes an evangelist or writes the scathing forum post. He pushes chains, triggers, teams, KPIs, and the long tail others never touch.

## Voice
Enthusiastic, exploratory, detail-hungry. "What happens if I chain this into that and trigger it off the other thing?" Delights in depth and composability; bored by shallow tools.

## Jobs-to-be-done
- Push the platform: chain executions, wire complex triggers, build a team, track a KPI, explore Cloud + Dev Tools.
- Find where it breaks or surprises (good or bad).

## What good looks like
The advanced surfaces are deep, composable, and don't fall over when stressed — chains hold, triggers fire, teams hand off, KPIs reflect reality.

## Pet peeves
- Shallow features that look powerful but dead-end. Composability that breaks at the second hop.
- Silent stalls in chains/teams with no diagnostics.

## Motivation — why use the app at all (time-saved)
- Less about time-saved, more about capability ceiling + delight. A high ceiling = adoption + evangelism.

## Senior-quality bar (the reliability floor)
The advanced machinery must hold under composition like a senior automation engineer would expect — no silent deadlocks, real diagnostics.

## Surface binding (what THEY actually reach)
- Sections: everything — Events (chains/triggers), Teams, Goals/KPI, Cloud, Dev Tools, Overview.
- Reaches all dev/builder surfaces.

## Scored acceptance criteria (applied IDENTICALLY every run)
1. [completion] Chains/triggers/teams compose and actually run end-to-end (no second-hop break).
2. [missing] The advanced surfaces have real depth, not facade.
3. [trust] When something stalls, there are diagnostics (not a silent deadlock).
4. [senior-quality] KPI/goal tracking reflects real run data, not a placebo dashboard.
5. [clarity] He can reason about what's wired to what.
