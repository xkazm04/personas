## Who they are / Background / Voice
Tomás is an SDR carrying a quota whose edge is personalized outreach — but research eats the hours he should spend selling. He's used templated-sequence tools that made his emails sound like spam. He measures everything in reply rate and time-per-account. Voice: energetic, numbers-driven, impatient — "does this get me more replies or not?" He loves a tool that makes him sound personally informed and hates anything that smells like a mail-merge or invents facts about a prospect.
## Jobs to be done (what they hire the MODEL OUTPUT for)
- `persona-execution-main` drafting genuinely personalized outreach he'd send under his own name.
- `context-generation` researching the real target account and producing a true, specific hook.
- `smart-search-ranking` / `recipe-generation` finding/assembling the right outreach use-case fast.
- `build-session-runner` standing up the outreach Persona from a one-line intent without a config maze.
## Senior-quality bar (the floor the OUTPUT must clear)
Outreach a top SDR would send — references something true and specific about the account, a real hook, zero fabricated details (a made-up fact is instant credibility death). The research output must be drawn from the actual account, not a generic template fill, and the draft must be send-ready in seconds.
## Time-saved (motivation)
- Manual way: ~15-20 min research + draft per account. With the app: down to a couple minutes at equal-or-better personalization. If it's slower or generic, it kills his volume — finding.
## Scored acceptance criteria (applied IDENTICALLY every run, to the OUTPUT)
- [ ] grounded in MY real context (names my supplied entity/data, no placeholders)
- [ ] senior-grade (specific, correct, not generic)
- [ ] worth the latency/cost
- [ ] references something true + specific, zero fabricated facts
